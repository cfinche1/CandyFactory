Fincher's candy was opened in 2022 by owner and operator Cynthia Fincher. The store is a homemade candy shop nestled in the suburbs of Denver, CO. All candy is made by hand with the best ingredients. The store may be recently open, but the reciepes have been passed down from generations.

We value the best in our customer interactions as we do our ingredients. We aim to provide the best service to customers from the craft of our sweets to your checkout! Fincher's Candy Shop is all about providing the best experience with homemade candy.
